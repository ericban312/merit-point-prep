About Merit Point Prep

Diagnostic-driven SAT prep, built around your student.

Merit Point Prep takes a personalized, diagnostic-driven approach to SAT preparation. Every student starts with a full-length diagnostic test that pinpoints exactly which concepts, question types, and pacing issues are holding their score back.

From there, we build a clear, custom study plan. No generic curriculum. No one-size-fits-all worksheets. Just focused practice on the areas that will move the needle most — taught by tutors who actually know the test inside and out.

The result? Students walk into test day confident, prepared, and ready to score higher than they thought possible.
